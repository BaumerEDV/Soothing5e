Soothing - an Additional Skill for 5e

Introduction

Often times adventurers find themselves in situations where monsters over eagerly attack or they themselves drew blades and flung spells too quickly: de-escalating such a situation is often awkward and arbitrary in normal course of play and the existing mechanics for Persuasion, and Intimidation do not quite match what the players are trying to do. It is for this reason, we are proud to introduce a new skill for less murderous player characters: Soothing.

How it Works

What Happens when Soothing is Added to the Campaign

Soothing is an additional Skill that is simply added to the list of available Skills in the game. It can be used for Ability Checks and you can gain Proficiency in it. Add it as a new entry in the skill list of all character sheets.

Soothing is added to the list of Skill Proficiencies players can pick from their class at Level 1 for the following Classes: Cleric, Monk, and Ranger.

If this Skill is added to an ongoing campaign, characters of these classes may exchange one of their Skill Proficiencies granted to them by their class for Soothing.

Naturally, Soothing is available as a Background Skill choice for every character - just like every other skill.

If Soothing is added to an ongoing campaign, characters may exchange one of their Skill Proficiencies gained from their Background for Soothing, provided they haven’t exchanged a Class Skill Proficiency for it already.

How it is Used

Soothing. Your Charisma (Soothing) check covers attempts to diffuse or stall tense situations, such as when combat is ongoing but you want to initiate a truce, or when someone is about to do something most unwise out of desperation. Targets of Soothing attempts are never treated as hostile for the purposes of finding a DC for the attempt and resolving it - this makes Soothing significantly more effective than other Charisma skills in this situation when following the expanded rules for social interaction. If Persuasion or Intimidation would also be appropriate for such a check and you are also Proficient in that appropriate skill, your check is made at Advantage.
